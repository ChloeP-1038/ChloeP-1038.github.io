Meet the Dragons

Explore the dragons bonded to the next generation of Viking riders.

Dragon Class Fire Type Personality Rider(s)
Toothless Strike Plasma Blast Toothless is intelligent, loyal, and fiercely protective. Though initially wary, he forms a deep emotional bond with Hiccup and demonstrates a playful, curious nature. His agility and stealth make him one of the most formidable dragons in the skies. Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III
Hookfang Stoker Monstrous Fire Hookfang is aggressive, proud, and a bit of a show-off. He enjoys setting himself on fire and asserting dominance, but he ultimately respects Snotlout’s leadership—when it suits him. Their bond is fiery in more ways than one. Snotlout Jorgenson
Stormfly Tracker Magnesium-Fueled Spines Stormfly is graceful, clever, and highly responsive to Astrid’s commands. She combines speed with precision and has a strong sense of loyalty. Her playful side emerges in quieter moments, especially when not under pressure. Astrid Hofferson
Meatlug Boulder Lava Ball Meatlug is gentle, affectionate, and surprisingly strong. She’s a cuddler at heart and adores Fishlegs, often responding to his voice with immediate affection. Despite her slow appearance, she’s a powerhouse in battle. Fishlegs Ingerman
Barf & Belch Hideous Zippleback Gas and Spark This two-headed dragon is chaotic, quirky, and constantly bickering with itself. Barf and Belch reflect the personalities of their riders, requiring teamwork and coordination to function. Their unique fire mechanism makes them unpredictable and dangerous. Ruffnut and Tuffnut Thorston
